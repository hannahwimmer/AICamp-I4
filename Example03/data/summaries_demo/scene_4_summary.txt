Eric Schmidt discusses the potential impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) on human lives.

Schmidt emphasizes that AI is not just a tool for automation but also has significant implications for society.

He believes that widespread adoption of AI will lead to both positive and negative outcomes, with many benefits such as improved healthcare and increased productivity.

However, Schmidt also notes that the potential risks associated with AI, including job displacement and bias, must be addressed carefully.

Ultimately, Schmidt concludes that a nuanced approach is necessary to harness the benefits of AI while mitigating its drawbacks.
